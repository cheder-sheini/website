Pushka Hub App

Support Cheder Sheini Daily!

Support our Shul

Support Cheder Sheini every day with the simple and secure Pushka Hub app!

By downloading this innovative app, you have the opportunity to fulfill the mitzvah of daily tzedakah with ease. The Pushka Hub app allows you to add our Shul as a beneficiary with just a few clicks, making it effortless to contribute towards our vital programs.

Pushka App Setup Instructions

1

Create an account on the Pushka Hub app by entering your full name, email address, and password.

Confirm your email address by inputting the code sent to your email.

2

Click "Add Pushka" and search for "Cheder Sheini" among the available pushkas.

3

Set the “Capacity” for your pushka, which serves as a threshold for automatic payments.

Once your pushka reaches this amount, your credit card will be charged, and the money will be transferred as a donation.

You are free to set this capacity to any amount you prefer.

4

Congratulations! You're all set! You are now ready to donate via the app. When the pushka is ready to be emptied you will be prompted to enter a credit card or select a digital wallet like Apple Pay.
